INTAKE STRUCTURE

→

The system is structured around three fixed intake points. Repeated consistently over a 72-hour emergency period. Each point is pre-timed to preserve a basic daily rhythm, regardless of external conditions.

How it works?
1

REGISTRATION

The user creates an account within the system to initiate the intake configuration process.

2

INITIAL SETTINGS

Basic information is entered, including age range, origin, and any relevant dietary restrictions such as allergies.

3

ROUTINE DEFINITION

A short sequence of questions is answered to define the user's eating routine across three days.

4

SYSTEM CALCULATION

The system processes the data to generate a full 72-hour intake structure based on stable conditions.

5

PATTERN ASSIGNMENT

The user receives a unique shape that encodes their intake profile. This visual pattern reflects their eating structure and becomes a reference for their emergency configuration.

6

FAMILY EXTENSION

Additional users can be added under the same account. Each receives their own intake configuration and visual pattern.

7

EMERGENCY DELIVERY

Once activated, pre-packed kits are shipped to the user's registered address for emergency use.

Developed during stable conditions, this system pre-defines meal timing, structure, and nutritional balance to ensure continuity when access is limited, disrupted, or uncertain. Preserving routine and reducing stress through pre-established logic. Established 2025.
